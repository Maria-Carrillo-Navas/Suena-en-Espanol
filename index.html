1-2-3 Dreaming in Spanish Podcast

A Spain-based B1–B2 podcast built on the “Same-scene x3” method: one small story, three escalating versions. Train your ear without losing context and finally bridge the gap between learner content and native speech.

How it works

  1. 1) Guided: a clear narrator frames the situation and previews vocabulary.
  2. 2) Slowed dialogue: the same scene with natural pauses and clean pronunciation.
  3. 3) Real conversation: authentic speed, interjections and colloquial Spanish from Spain.

Perfect for busy learners who want meaningful listening, not mindless drills.
